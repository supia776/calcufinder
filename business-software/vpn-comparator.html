VPN Comparator

A high-level overview of well-known VPN providers and what they tend to focus on — streaming, privacy, remote work, or a mix of all three. Use this as a starting point and always double-check details on each VPN’s official website.

Category: Business & software Audience: Remote workers, travellers, privacy-conscious users

How to think about VPN choices

Different VPNs emphasise different things: some lean toward speed and streaming, others toward strong privacy policies.

Server locations and local regulations can affect what you can do, and which VPNs are available.

Prices below are described in broad ranges and usually in USD, because many VPN providers list plans in USD on their sites. You can estimate costs in your own currency using the Currency Converter calculator on CalcuFinder.

VPN legality and acceptable usage can vary by country. Always follow the laws and regulations where you live or travel, and review each provider’s terms of service carefully.

VPN providers overview

NordVPN
Balanced: speed, streaming, and security

Widely known VPN provider with a large server network, strong encryption, and options for streaming, remote work, and general browsing privacy.

Popular choice Streaming & remote work No-logs policy (per provider claims)

Typical range: multi-year plans often average in the lower to mid tens of USD per month when broken down, with shorter terms usually costing more.

Premium, speed-focused VPN

Known for fast connections, wide server coverage, and ease of use across devices. Often positioned as a premium option for streaming and secure browsing.

Speed & reliability Streaming-friendly Multi-device support

Typical range: considered a higher-priced VPN, with plans often sitting in the mid tens of USD per month before discounts.

Surfshark
Budget-friendly with unlimited devices

VPN with competitive pricing, unlimited device connections on many plans, and features that appeal to families and people with multiple devices.

Budget-friendly Unlimited devices (plan dependent) Streaming & everyday use

Typical range: multi-year deals can bring the effective monthly cost into the lower tens of USD, with monthly plans priced higher.

Privacy-focused provider

Privacy emphasis Secure core options Free & paid tiers

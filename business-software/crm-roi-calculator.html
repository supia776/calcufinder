CRM ROI Calculator

Get a rough sense of whether your CRM or customer platform is paying for itself. This helper looks at extra conversions, customer value, and retention compared with the monthly cost of your CRM.

Category: Business & software Audience: Online businesses & teams

Inputs

How many leads, enquiries, or potential deals you typically see in a month.

Approximate percentage of leads that become paying customers now.

A realistic guess for extra conversion from better follow-up, automation, and tracking (for example +2–5%).

Use the typical first-order or first-month revenue, in your own currency.

Rough estimate of how long an average customer stays active or keeps buying.

Include seats, add-ons, and taxes if they are part of the monthly bill.

This tool gives an approximate view only and ignores many real-world factors (churn patterns, payment failures, seasonality, and more).

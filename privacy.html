Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how CalcuFinder handles information when you visit the site, use the tools, or contact the project.

1. What data CalcuFinder collects

CalcuFinder is designed to be simple and lightweight. In general, you can use the calculators without creating an account or logging in.

CalcuFinder may collect:

  • Information you voluntarily provide – for example, when you send an email, submit a contact form, or subscribe to a newsletter, you may share your name, email address, and the content of your message.
  • Basic technical information – such as your browser type, device type, and approximate region, typically collected via standard web server logs or analytics tools.

The numbers you type into calculators (such as income, savings, or loan amounts) are processed in your browser to produce results. CalcuFinder does not attempt to link these values to your identity.

2. Cookies and analytics

CalcuFinder may use cookies or similar technologies to understand how people use the site and to improve the tools over time.

  • Cookies may be used to remember basic preferences, such as language or previously selected options.
  • Analytics tools (for example, privacy-aware web analytics) may collect aggregated, anonymised usage data to see which pages and tools are most used.

CalcuFinder does not use analytics to build personal advertising profiles or to track individuals across unrelated websites.

3. How your information is used

Information may be used to:

  • Reply to messages or questions you send via email or contact form.
  • Improve calculators, explanations, and user experience over time.
  • Monitor site reliability, security, and performance.

CalcuFinder does not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Email and newsletters

If you subscribe to updates or newsletters, your email address will be used to send you occasional messages about new tools, articles, or relevant financial topics.

  • You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in the email (once enabled).
  • Email addresses provided for newsletters or contact forms are not shared with third parties for their own marketing.

5. Third-party links and services

CalcuFinder may include links to external websites, tools, or services (for example, banks, insurers, SaaS tools, or content platforms).

These sites have their own privacy policies and data practices. You should always review the privacy and security information on any external website before sharing personal details or making financial decisions.

6. Data security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect the website and any stored information from unauthorised access, misuse, or loss. However, no online service can guarantee perfect security.

You should avoid sending highly sensitive personal information (such as full identification numbers, full account details, or passwords) through contact forms or email.

7. Children’s privacy

CalcuFinder is intended for adults and does not target children. If you believe information about a child has been provided by mistake, you can request to be deleted using the Contact page.

8. Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time as the project grows or as legal requirements change. The “last updated” date will be adjusted when significant changes are made.

9. How to contact CalcuFinder about privacy

If you have questions or concerns about privacy, you can reach out via the Contact page or by email at info@calcufinder.com.

Some pages on CalcuFinder may include affiliate links. If you click and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This helps support the project and keeps the tools free to use.

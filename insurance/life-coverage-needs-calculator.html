Life Coverage Needs Calculator

Get a rough estimate of how much life insurance cover you may want, based on your income, debts, savings, and dependants. This is a starting point only – not personal financial advice.

Category: Life insurance Audience: Individuals & families worldwide

Inputs

Used only for context notes – this tool does not look up real products or tax rules.

Enter the approximate amount your household actually receives from your income each year, in your local currency.

Common ranges are 5–10 years when children are young, or until major debts are cleared.

Include major debts you would like to see fully or mostly repaid, in the same currency as income.

Include emergency fund and accessible investments that could support your household.

Include any standalone life policies, plus death cover inside pensions/super, if relevant.

This helper uses simple, approximate logic. It cannot account for every factor such as tax, investment returns, or future changes in your life.

Results

Add your income, debts, savings, and dependants to see a rough coverage estimate in your local currency.
